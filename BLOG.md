# Automate Tron Blockchain Operations with Our New n8n Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-tron, a custom community node that brings Tron blockchain functionality directly into your n8n workflows!

## The Challenge

As blockchain technology continues to mature, businesses are increasingly integrating cryptocurrency operations into their workflows. However, connecting blockchain functionality with business automation tools has traditionally required custom code, API wrappers, and significant development effort. For teams working with Tron (TRX) and TRC-20 tokens, this has meant manual processes or expensive custom integrations.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-tron

Velocity BPA has developed n8n-nodes-tron to bridge this gap. This community node enables you to seamlessly interact with the Tron blockchain directly from your n8n automation workflows—no coding required.

## Key Features

Our node provides comprehensive Tron blockchain functionality:

- **TRX Transfers**: Send and receive TRX with simple workflow nodes
- **TRC-20 Token Support**: Interact with any TRC-20 token, including transfers and balance queries
- **Account Queries**: Check account balances, transaction history, and account information
- **Real-time Operations**: Execute blockchain transactions as part of your automated workflows

## Use Cases

Imagine automating cryptocurrency payment processing, triggering business workflows based on blockchain events, or integrating token operations with your CRM, accounting software, or customer communications. The possibilities are extensive.

## Getting Started

Installation is straightforward. In your n8n instance, navigate to Settings > Community Nodes and search for "n8n-nodes-tron", or install via npm:


npm install n8n-nodes-tron


Once installed, you'll find the Tron node available in your node palette, ready to drag into your workflows.
